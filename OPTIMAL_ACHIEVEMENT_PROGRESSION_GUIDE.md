## üí° KEY INSIGHTS FOR OPTIMAL PLAY

1. **Play on Rookie Difficulty** - 2x energy regen, higher obstacle frequency, lower collision range
   - Makes single-session unlocks 2-3x easier
   - Allows farming grinds faster

2. **Master Early Skills First** - Ground Pound, Houdini, Fire Spinner open all other paths
   - Ground Pound ‚Üí Fiery Ground Pound ‚Üí Fire Stomper (chain)
   - Incinerations ‚Üí Fiery Houdini, Firestorm, Mage Spinner (parallel paths)

3. **Parallel Farming Strategy** - Track multiple counters simultaneously
   - Playing one game can progress 3-4 different unlocks
   - Example: One game = 1 toward Fire Stomper + 3 toward Echo Slam + 5 toward Shotgun

4. **Skill vs. Grind Balance** - Skill-based unlocks (Blink Strike, Jet Pack) early, grind-based later
   - Skill-based: 1-2 attempts = done (Fiery Ground Pound, Blink Strike)
   - Grind-based: 25-100 attempts = done (Tarzan, Reaper Drone)

5. **Time Investment Reality** - Total to get everything: 200-400 hours
   - But most of that is background grind (Tarzan/Reaper)
   - 80% of skills in first 10 hours
   - Last 20% takes 190+ hours (endgame only)
